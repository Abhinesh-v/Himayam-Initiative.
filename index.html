The Hidden Crisis in Indian Education

  • ✓

    67% of 12th pass students in rural Tamil Nadu cannot afford college

  • ✓

    ₹2.5 lakhs average annual college cost vs. ₹40,000 average family income

  • ✓

    89% of these students end up in unstable, low-paying jobs

  • ✓

    Result: India loses millions of potential professionals every year

"Behind every statistic is a student who had to choose between family survival and personal dreams."
